What is hidden in Hidden Markov Models (HMM)?
  • Can't measure air temp 1000 years ago -- Hidden
  • Can inspect tree growth rings formed then -- Observed

Today, we can't measure air temp 1000 years ago. However, air temp back then affected trees growth. We can see its impact on trees growth by inspecting their growth rings. - Light ring is spring, dark ring is summer. - Number of dark rings = tree age in years
